Who is Alec?

Alec was born in Atlanta, Georgia during the year 1993. He grew up in Roswell, Georgia where he attended Sequoyah High School and Graduated in 2011. His heritage is Thai and Lao.

After graduating high school, he attended Georgia State University where he obtaine a degree in Economics. He has worked in the Restaurant and Real Estate industries respectively.

During his spare time he loves to exercise, code, read and explore new areas. He loves to travel to different countries and has personally been to 10 countries. His favorite city he has traveled to is Florence, Italy.
